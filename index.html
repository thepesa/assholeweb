About Us

Hello, we are Software, Cyber Security and IoT Software Archive Welcome to Discord Server! This server was established to share the most up-to-date, quality and useful resources about software, cyber security and IoT software and to bring together people who have or want to have knowledge in these fields. What can you find on our server? You can find articles, ebooks, video lectures, podcasts, webinars, events and more on software, cybersecurity and IoT software. You can join the discussion channels to improve yourself in these areas, learn new things, ask and answer your questions. You can meet people who work or want to work in these fields, network, browse or share job postings. In these areas, you can present the projects you have done or want to do, get feedback, search or find partners. You also have many options to have fun on our server. With game channels, bots, emojis, roles, tags and more, you can learn and have fun on our server. What is the purpose, vision and mission of our server? The purpose of our server is to bring together people who are interested in software, cyber security and IoT software, to share information in these areas and to contribute to these areas. The vision of our server is to be Turkey's largest, most active and highest quality software, cyber security and IoT software community. The mission of our server is to support the success of everyone who wants to improve themselves in these areas by providing equal opportunities. When you join our server, please introduce yourself and indicate your areas of interest. We will give you the appropriate roles and tags. Also read and confirm server rules. Welcome to our server! We hope you have a pleasant time on our server and get useful information. If you have any questions, suggestions or complaints, please contact the server administrators. He is happy to assist you.
